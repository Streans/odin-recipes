Curry Recipe

Description

A classic curry with a rich and aromatic sauce, served with rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken, diced
  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup curry powder
  • 1 can coconut milk

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the diced chicken and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in the curry powder and cook for another minute.
  4. pour in the coconut milk and bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
