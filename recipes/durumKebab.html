Durum Kebab Recipe

Description

A classic durum kebab with seasoned meat, vegetables, and a special sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 4 lettuce leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Form the ground beef into 4 patties and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill the patties for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until cooked to your liking.
  4. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill for 30 seconds.
  5. Assemble the burgers by placing a patty on each bun and topping with lettuce, tomato, and onion.
